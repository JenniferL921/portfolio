Projects


Weather Or Not App

Emoti-Con NYC Youth Digital Media & Technology Challenge of 2023


Weather Or Not provides information about different kinds of weather and gives suggestion on what to prepare for a specific weather. Users can select a weather condition that they want to learn about and the information will be displayed on the screen. Then the user can click on the “more” button for advice.

NYC Connect Us App

Infy App Challenge of 2024 & Emoti-Con NYC Youth Digital Media & Technology Challenge of 2024


Created by a team of 5, Team FDS, and with the assistance from 2 mentors, the purpose of NYC Connect Us is to find missing persons and help them reunite with their families and friends. It allows users to publish a post about the missing person and communicate with the agents to help with the investigation. Users can also access resources such as food and shelter through charites.

Cloud Closet App

Congressional App Challenge of 2024


Created by Team CC, Michelle and I, Cloud Closet aims to help anyone who needs outfit suggestions based on weather. Cloud Closet provides a weather forecast, appropriate outfit recommendations based on weather from our Chatbot Ruki, and a "closet" where users can mix-and-match their existing clothes on their character.
